Classic Pancakes

There's nothing quite like a stack of fluffy, golden brown pancakes.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted

Preparation

  1. In a large bowl sift together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, egg, and melted butter
  3. Pour thw wet ingredients into the dry ingredients amd stir until just combined. A few lumps are fine. Do not overmix
  4. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat.
  5. Pour about 1/4 cup of the batter onto the griddle for each pancake
  6. Cook until bubbles appear on the surface of the pancake, then flip and cook until golden brown on the other side.
  7. Serve immediately with your favorite toppings.
